The Exploration of MARS!

  • High Concept: This is a platformer in which you are the first explorer dropped off at MARS. Your mission is simple, explore the Martian landscape and possibly find aliens!
  • Player Experience Goals: This is a game made for the 'explorer' types (as the title denotes) and the main goal is simply to explore the landscape. It is more of a side-scroller type game so it isn't a true overhead scroller like most explorer games.
  • Genre: Platformer
  • Game play: The Game play is fairly straightforward and you use the arrow keys to control your ship. Collect any gems that show up and avoid touching the lava!
  • Prototype goal: 1) Platformer: Haven't made one of these yet 2) Side-scroller
  • Features: Contrasting display (I think aesthetics are a feature as much as anything else)
  • Setting/Premise: You are the first explorer dropped off at Mars to explore the Martian terrain. Explore!
  • Story: While NASA promised you they would get you back to Earth, the deeper you explore the more you start to wonder if their promises are true..
  • Target Audience: Explorers
  • Play time: 3-5 minutes
  • Strategies: No real strategies, just avoid the baddies.
  • Prototype Assets: All assets were cloned directly from phasers examples: https://github.com/photonstorm/phaser-examples/tree/master/examples/assets/sprites or made by me in paint
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: The game we all played as kids where you were a martian and have a ship where you collect gems. I can't remember the name
